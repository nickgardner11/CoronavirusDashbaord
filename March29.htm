A second person dies from COVID-19 in King County

NBC New York

Two People in NYC Being Tested for COVID-19: Officials


Two people in New York City are being tested for COVID-19, more commonly known as the novel coronavirus, the city's Health Department said Sunday. The two people traveled to the city from Iran, a spokesperson for the department told News 4.

World News

XinhuaNet: Shanghai uses 5G robots on frontline to combat COVID-19


On the frontline of the COVID-19 battle, six smart robots using 5G technology have been working side by side with medics in Shanghai. The robots are used for tasks such as disinfecting and cleaning patient areas and delivering medicine to reduce the risk of cross-infection and ensure the safety of medics.
